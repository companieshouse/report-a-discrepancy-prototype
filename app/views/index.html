Changes this version

  • 'What does the discrepancy you are reporting relate to?' screen brought back into prototype. The screen which routed the users dependent onn whether they typed a company number or overseas entity id did not test well. Therefore moving back to asking this question to route down either journey.

  • Guidence page changed to give more emphasis to when a user can report a discrepancy and move the content related to the changes from 1st April to the top

Prototype data

  • To access PSC screens from 'What is the company number or Overseas Entity ID?' screen use any 8 digit number

  • To access Beneficial owner screens from 'What is the company number or Overseas Entity ID?' screen use 'oe' followed by any 6 numbers

  • To access secure PSC screen from 'What is the company number or Overseas Entity ID?' screen use - 00000000

  • To access secure PSC screenfrom 'What is the company number or Overseas Entity ID?' screen use - oe000000

  • Sign in screen moved to front of service
